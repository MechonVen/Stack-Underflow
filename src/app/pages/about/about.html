Our Story

The Zonta Club of Naples was founded in 1990 by a group of dedicated women who envisioned a community where women could support one another, advocate for equality, and make tangible differences in the lives of other women. What started as a small gathering of 15 members has grown into a thriving organization of over 200 women committed to our mission.

Affiliated with Zonta International, a global organization of professionals working to advance the status of women worldwide, the Naples club has become a beacon of hope and opportunity for women in our region. Through scholarships, mentorship, advocacy, and community service, we continue to break barriers and create pathways for women to thrive.

Every member brings unique talents, experiences, and perspectives that enrich our organization. Together, we believe in the power of women supporting women and are committed to leaving a lasting legacy of positive change.
