Membership Requirements

The Zonta Club of Naples welcomes professional women who are committed to our mission of empowering women and girls. Prospective members should:

  • Be a professional woman with a record of achievement in their career or community
  • Demonstrate a commitment to advancing the status of women
  • Be willing to actively participate in club activities and service projects
  • Attend regular club meetings and events
  • Pay annual membership dues
  • Uphold the values and mission of Zonta International

Membership Types

Active Membership

Full membership with voting rights and the opportunity to hold office. Active members participate in all club activities and contribute to decision-making.

Honorary Membership

Recognizes individuals who have made significant contributions to Zonta's mission. Honorary members are exempt from dues but may participate in club activities.
