Service

Adelle Hunter, Chairperson

Zonta International advocates for women's human rights and gender equality in all areas of women's rights. ZI partners with UN agencies like UNFPA, U.S. Fund UNICEF which financially support projects to empower women and girls. The projects focus on issues; health, education, equality and human rights, human trafficking etc.

At the local club levels the clubs are functioning at one hundred percent. The club projects focus on women and girls issues. Zonta Club of Naples service committee is responsible for finding out what the needs are for the community. This committee meets monthly to discuss projects and makes recommendations to the board. The committee is to coordinate those projects approved in which all members are to participate in the activities and events.

Our Service Projects

🎒

Back to School Drive

Every year, we collect and distribute school supplies to underprivileged students in Collier County, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed academically.

👗

Dress for Success

We partner with local organizations to provide professional attire and career development resources to women entering or re-entering the workforce.

🏠

Shelter Support

Our members volunteer at local women's shelters, providing support, resources, and mentorship to women and families in transition.

📚

Literacy Programs

We support literacy initiatives that help women and girls develop reading skills and access educational opportunities.

🌍

International Service

Through Zonta International, we support global projects that combat human trafficking, improve maternal health, and promote education for girls worldwide.

💼

Mentorship Programs

We connect professional women with students and young professionals, providing guidance, support, and career development opportunities.
