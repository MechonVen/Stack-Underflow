Scholarships

Supporting Young Women in Their Educational Journey

The Zonta Club of Naples is committed to empowering young women through education. We offer scholarships to help deserving students pursue their academic and career goals.

Available Scholarships

Young Women in Public Affairs (YWPA) Award

This award recognizes young women, ages 16-19, who demonstrate a commitment to leadership, volunteer service, and empowerment of women worldwide.

  • Award Amount: Up to $5,000
  • Eligibility: Women ages 16-19
  • Deadline: March 1st annually

Jane M. Klausman Women in Business Scholarship

Supporting women pursuing undergraduate or master's degrees in business management who demonstrate outstanding potential in the field.

  • Award Amount: Up to $8,000
  • Eligibility: Women in business programs
  • Deadline: April 1st annually

Local Naples Scholarship

Our club offers local scholarships to Collier County students who demonstrate academic excellence and commitment to community service.

  • Award Amount: Varies
  • Eligibility: Collier County residents
  • Deadline: May 1st annually

How to Apply

1

Carefully read the eligibility criteria for each scholarship program.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather transcripts, letters of recommendation, and personal statements.

3

Submit Application

Complete and submit your application before the deadline.
