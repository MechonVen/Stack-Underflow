Advocacy

Championing Women's Rights and Gender Equality

Advocacy is a cornerstone of Zonta's mission. We work at local, national, and international levels to advance the status of women and promote gender equality through policy change and public awareness.

Our Advocacy Focus Areas

🚫 Ending Gender-Based Violence

We advocate for stronger laws and support systems to protect women and girls from domestic violence, human trafficking, and other forms of gender-based violence.

  • Support for survivors of domestic violence
  • Anti-trafficking initiatives
  • Legal reform and protection measures

⚖️ Equal Rights and Opportunities

We champion equal pay, workplace equality, and women's representation in leadership positions across all sectors of society.

  • Pay equity campaigns
  • Women in leadership initiatives
  • Anti-discrimination policies

🏥 Maternal and Reproductive Health

We advocate for access to quality healthcare, including maternal health services and reproductive rights for all women.

  • Maternal health programs
  • Access to healthcare services
  • Health education and awareness

🎓 Education Access

We work to ensure that all girls have access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

  • STEM education for girls
  • Scholarship programs
  • Removing barriers to education

Current Campaigns

16 Days of Activism

An annual international campaign to end gender-based violence, running from November 25 (International Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women) to December 10 (Human Rights Day).

Zonta Says NO

Our global campaign to end violence against women, raising awareness and advocating for policy changes to protect women and girls.

Equal Pay Day

We participate in Equal Pay Day activities to highlight the gender pay gap and advocate for pay equity legislation.
